# FM-2 Financial Mathematics

## Exam Format
- **Number of Questions**: 30 multiple-choice questions
- **Time Limit**: 2.5 hours (150 minutes)
- **Format**: Computer-Based Testing (CBT) via Prometric
- **Calculator**: TI-30XS MultiView or BA II Plus (strongly recommended for interest rate calculations)
- **Passing Score**: Scaled score of 6 out of 10 (approximately 71% correct)
- **Frequency**: Offered year-round at Prometric centers

## Exam Topics

The exam covers fundamental concepts of financial mathematics and their application:
- Time value of money
- Annuities (immediate, due, perpetuities)
- Loans and mortgages
- Bonds and bond pricing
- Yield curves
- Duration and convexity
- Immunization
- Interest rate sensitivity
